Book Club for Brettknee

The Tainted Cup

by Robert Jackson Bennett

📄 432 Pages 🏷️ Fantasy Mystery

In an empire threatened by monstrous leviathans, an eccentric investigator and her magically enhanced assistant unravel a murder that exposes a conspiracy capable of bringing the empire to its knees.

Dark Places

by Gillian Flynn

📄 352 Pages 🏷️ Psychological Thriller

Haunted by the murder of her family as a child, Libby Day reluctantly revisits the infamous case when a group of amateur investigators questions whether the convicted killer was truly guilty.

The Other Passenger

by Louise Candlish

📄 384 Pages 🏷️ Psychological Thriller

A London commuter's life unravels when his daily travel companion disappears and he becomes the prime suspect, forcing him to uncover the truth before it's too late.

The Hobbit

by J.R.R. Tolkien

📄 310 Pages 🏷️ Fantasy

Bilbo Baggins, a comfort-loving hobbit, is swept into an unexpected adventure with the wizard Gandalf and a company of dwarves on a quest to reclaim their homeland and treasure from the fearsome dragon Smaug.
